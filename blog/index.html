Apple launches Creator Studio

Apple launched Creator Studio today for iPad and Mac today for users who are looking to take advantage of all Apple’s pro level apps all in one subscription. This new subscription is available for individuals (with up to 5 family members using Family Share) and Students.


The Individual Subscription is $12.99/month or $129/year or $2.99/month or $29/year for Students. The suite includes the following pro apps.

  • Final Cut Pro
  • Motion
  • Compressor
  • Logic Pro
  • MainStage
  • Pixelmator Pro

It also includes new templates and features for the following productivity apps

  • Numbers
  • Pages
  • Keynote
  • Freeform

It is available now in the App Store, and Apple is offering a 30-day trial for those who are wishing to take advantage of it.

We messed up

Remember when we said we were going to move everything to Notion and host all of our content over there and that is was going to be easier to push out new content? Well that kind of backfired spectactularly. Put simply, we oversold and under-delivered on our commitment to make Notion our new platform and we decided to revert back to the GitHub project instead.


So here's the deal. We are putting the finishing touches on the new website, and we'll bring over the content over the course of this week. There wasn't a lot of content, so the transition should be pretty smooth. With that being said, we are revising our internal practices and will adjust accrdingly so that this mistakes like this don't happen again.
