Apple launches Creator Studio

Apple launched Creator Studio today for iPad and Mac today for users who are looking to take advantage of all Apple’s pro level apps all in one subscription. This new subscription is available for individuals (with up to 5 family members using Family Share) and Students.


The Individual Subscription is $12.99/month or $129/year or $2.99/month or $29/year for Students. The suite includes the following pro apps.

  • - Final Cut Pro
  • - Motion
  • - Compressor
  • - Logic Pro
  • - MainStage
  • - Pixelmator Pro

It also includes new templates and features for the following productivity apps

  • - Numbers
  • - Pages
  • - Keynote
  • - Freeform

It is available now in the App Store, and Apple is offering a 30-day trial for those who are wishing to take advantage of it.
