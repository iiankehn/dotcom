Coldstone Platform Update (16.0.21231 - March 30, 2024)

Posted on Saturday, March 30, 2024 by Iian Kehn

I am excited to announce the first public preview of Coldstone CMS. This release brings a lot of welcome additions to the front-end of the website.

Enhanced User Experience:

  • This release delivers a completely revamped user experience designed specifically for both mobile devices and desktops.
  • We've listened to your feedback and addressed many of your concerns, making the platform more intuitive and enjoyable to use.

Customization:

  • Browser Theming: Enjoy an interface that adapts to your browser's theme preferences.
  • System Color Support: The platform now adapts to your system's light or dark mode for a seamless and consistent experience.

Bug Fixes and Improvements:

  • This release also includes various bug fixes and general improvements to enhance stability and performance.
