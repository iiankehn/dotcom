About Sevan Core

Sevan Core is a technology and blog that focuses on consumer technology, homelab and IT.

We started Sevan Core (previously Sevan) in 2005 covering technology news, guides, reviews and more. In 2023, we closed our doors to shift our focus and relaunch in 2025 as a content creation hub for all things tech.

Our mission is to provide high-quality, reliable information and resources to help our readers and viewers make informed decisions about technology. Whether you're a tech enthusiast, a professional, or an average consumer.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. We look forward to sharing our passion for technology with you!
